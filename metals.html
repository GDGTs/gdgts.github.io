Inductively Coupled Plasma - Mass Spectrometry

Mass spectrometry is a powerful analytical technique for identifying chemicals in a sample. Mass spectrometry relies on magnetic forces to influence the trajectory of ions traveling through it, where the mass of the ion determines its path through the magnetic field.
